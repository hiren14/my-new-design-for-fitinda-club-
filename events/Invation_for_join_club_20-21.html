Government Engineering College Patan invites applications from students for membership of the Fit India Youth Club for actively participation and different Committees of the College for the Management of fitness related activities, sports & cultural Events, celebration of different days, different programs & functions at the College, as per the circular of Government & GTU.

Eligibility criteria

Any student of any year who is a current student of Government engineering college Patan. And should be is regular in class.

Student only required to active participation. 

There is no restriction for students who have any backlog; student who is enthusiastic for joining can apply.

Link for apply:  http://bit.ly/JOIN-FIYCGECP   

Link Role & Responsibility & SOP please Read this PDF:  http://bit.ly/FIYC-SOP


NOTE:

All students who want to be a class representative, committee member, committee head, committee secretary, coordinator are compulsory require to join fit India youth club. The selection of all committee members will under the supervision of the faculty coordinator of Fit India Youth Club & Gymkhana Vice president. The selection of all Committee members and leaders is purely based on seniority, interest, talent & experience. The current committee will arrange skill sessions, & interviews for the selections.

For this please read SOP.

This committee & membership will effective from April 2021 to March 2022.

After the active participation of one year, all members will be rewarded by the certificate of respective membership as per their contribution. It can add value to your resume and also useful for the Activity point subject.

After this day regarding this, all updates will be upload on the FIYC blog so please regularly check our BlogSpot.
